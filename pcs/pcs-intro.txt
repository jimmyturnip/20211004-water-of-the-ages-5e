| WHO ARE THE PCS?
|------------------------------------------
*/

Turlust is a human fighter and ex-mercenary. He works as a guard for the merchant house.

Laeral is a half-elf sorcerer. She is a friend of Turlust and Himdri, who both appreciate her magickal skills.

Himdri is a dwarf earth cleric who works as a craftsman for the merchant house.
